# Project title
AI-Based Dropout Prediction and Counselling System
# Overview
The AI-Powered Student Dropout Prediction and Counseling System is a web-based application designed to help educational institutions identify students who are at risk of dropping out. The system analyzes student data such as attendance, academic performance, and engagement using Machine Learning techniques to predict dropout risk. Based on the prediction results, personalized counseling recommendations are provided to support students and improve retention rates.
# Problem Statement
Many educational institutions rely on manual observation and traditional academic monitoring methods to identify students who may discontinue their studies. These approaches are often reactive and may fail to detect early warning signs of dropout. As a result, institutions may lose opportunities to provide timely support and intervention. There is a need for an intelligent system that can analyze student data, predict dropout risk at an early stage, and recommend appropriate counseling measures to improve student success and retention.
# Project Objectives
1.Predict students who are at risk of dropping out by analyzing attendance, academic performance, and other relevant factors using Machine Learning algorithms.  
2.Provide early warning alerts and risk assessments to faculty members and counselors, enabling timely intervention and support for at-risk students.  
3.Generate personalized counseling recommendations based on individual student performance and risk factors to improve academic success and retention.  
4.Monitor and track student progress through dashboards, reports, and performance analytics, helping institutions make data-driven decisions.  
5.Improve overall student retention and educational outcomes by combining predictive analytics, counseling support, and continuous performance monitoring within a single intelligent system.
