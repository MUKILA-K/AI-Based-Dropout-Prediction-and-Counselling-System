# Project title
AI-Based Dropout Prediction and Counselling System
# Problem Overview
Educational institutions face challenges in identifying students who are at risk of dropping out before the situation becomes critical. Traditional methods rely on manual monitoring of attendance, academic performance, and student behavior by faculty members and class advisors. However, with a large number of students, it becomes difficult to continuously analyze student data and detect hidden patterns that may indicate a potential dropout risk.The AI-Based Dropout Prediction and Counseling System uses Machine Learning techniques to analyze student data such as attendance, academic performance, assignment completion, and other relevant factors. The system predicts the likelihood of student dropout and provides personalized counseling recommendations, enabling institutions to take proactive measures to improve student retention and academic success.
# Problem Statement
Many educational institutions struggle to identify students who are at risk of dropping out in a timely manner. Existing monitoring methods are often manual, time-consuming, and unable to detect complex patterns across large datasets. As a result, students facing academic, personal, or behavioral challenges may not receive the necessary support before leaving their studies.The proposed system aims to develop an intelligent platform that predicts dropout risks using Machine Learning algorithms and assists faculty and counselors by providing early warnings, analytical insights, and personalized counseling recommendations.

 # Project Objectives
    1.Predict students who are at risk of dropping out by analyzing attendance, academic performance, and other relevant factors using Machine Learning algorithms.
    2.Provide early warning alerts and risk assessments to faculty members and counselors, enabling timely intervention and support for at-risk students.
    3.Generate personalized counseling recommendations based on individual student performance and risk factors to improve academic success and retention.
    4.Monitor and track student progress through dashboards, reports, and performance analytics, helping institutions make data-driven decisions.
    5.Improve overall student retention and educational outcomes by combining predictive analytics, counseling support, and continuous performance monitoring within a single intelligent system.
